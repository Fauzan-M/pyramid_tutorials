==========================
Pyramid Add-On Development
==========================

Pyramid has many strengths. In particular, Pyramid excels at being a
framework-framework: a pluggable system where you can build custom
pluggable system for your developers.

This series of tutorial sections demonstrate some of the features you
can use when making add-ons that extend Pyramid and provide custom plug
points for other developers.
